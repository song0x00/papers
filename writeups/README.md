# writeups/

Practical writeups from real-world engagements and self-directed lab work. Distinct from CTF writeups in format and intent.

---

## Subdirectories

### [`bug-bounty/`](./bug-bounty/)
Documented vulnerability findings from bug bounty programs. Includes scope, reproduction steps, impact assessment, and disclosure timeline where applicable.

> Only include findings that have been **triaged, resolved, or publicly disclosed** by the program. Check program rules before publishing.

### [`lab/`](./lab/)
Self-contained lab environment writeups — home lab configs, isolated exploit testing, and research that doesn't fit the bug bounty or CTF format.

---
